Late Night Relaxation

This video was taken back when I first got my license. I remember I wanted to drive, nowhere in particular but just wanted to drive anywhere. It was just so meditating to me. I just put on my music in my car and drove late at night to wherever I wanted. It was also around the time when my grandma died so I just wanted to be alone for a while. I think driving around Oakland was a great way as a younger individual to release my stress and anxiety. It was like I was able to take a fresh breath of air. As to why I recorded this? Well i've seen many youtubers who recorded themselves driving late at night and many people found it relaxing and meditating, so I just wanted to do the same and see how it goes.
